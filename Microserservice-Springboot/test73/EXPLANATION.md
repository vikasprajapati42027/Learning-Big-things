# Test 73: Elasticsearch (Full-Text Search)

Finding clear answers in messy data.

## Key Files:
- `SearchService.java`: Demonstrates the concept of searching documents.
- `EXPLANATION.md`: Fuzzy search, highlighting, and relevance scoring.

## Why use it?
Traditional SQL searches (`LIKE %...%`) are incredibly slow on millions of rows. Elasticsearch uses an "Inverted Index" (like the index in the back of a book) to find any word in milliseconds, regardless of how much data you have.
