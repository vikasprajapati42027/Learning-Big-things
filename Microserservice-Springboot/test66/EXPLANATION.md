High-throughput event streaming.

## Key Files:
- `KafkaProducerService.java`: Shows how to send messages to a Kafka Topic.
- `EXPLANATION.md`: Why Kafka is the backbone of big data systems.

## Why use it?
Kafka is like a giant, indestructible digital tape recorder. You can record millions of events per second, and any service can replay them at their own speed. It's the standard for Event-Driven Microservices.
